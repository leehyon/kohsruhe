But when I saw those stocks that had continuous daily limit ups, I thought maybe I could make some profits if I had a better understanding of this concept. What is Infono

“Infono” refers to the innovation industry of information technology application, which aims to achieve autonomous and controllable information technology and ensure national information security. Its core lies in the construction of a domestically-produced information technology software and hardware underlying architecture system and a full-cycle ecological system through industry application promotion, solving the “bottleneck” problem of core technology key links. The “Infono” industry is the foundation of the digital economy and the development of information security, and is also an important content of “new infrastructure”. It will become one of the important means to promote China’s economic growth.

Before discussing “Infono”, let’s first talk about the big picture.

“Infono” is not actually a new concept, but has recently sparked market discussions with policy catalysis. The importance of “Infono” is self-evident. As early as 2016, the country mentioned the security and controllability of the information industry. Since then, the concept of “Infono” has gradually expanded to the entire industrialization process from bottom-layer chips to upper-layer applications.

The narrow definition of “Infono” refers to the things that support the needs of party and government offices, such as office software and databases, which are the “2” in the “2+8+N” system proposed by the government. Currently, this node of “Infono” has developed into a broader concept, involving “8+N”, including finance, telecommunications, electricity and transportation, and other industries. It not only refers to the general software and hardware that support office work but also involves many aspects such as the localization of core components.

The concept of “Infono” fermented from 2019 to 2020, completing the process from 0 to 1. Looking forward from the current node, “Infono” should have a larger expectation both in terms of time and space.
